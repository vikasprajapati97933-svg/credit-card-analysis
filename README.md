# 💳 Credit Card Financial & Customer Analysis Dashboard
## 🚀 Business Intelligence Dashboard using Power BI & DAX

---

## 📖 Project Overview

This project delivers a **360-degree analysis** of credit card operations, combining transaction data with customer demographics to uncover key business insights.

By integrating two datasets, the project identifies:

- 💎 High-value customer segments
- 💸 Spending behavior patterns
- 📍 Revenue drivers across regions and card types

💡 The goal is to transform raw data into **actionable insights** that support business decision-making.

---

## 📂 Dataset Description

### 1. 💳 Credit Card Dataset
Contains transaction-level data:

| Feature | Details |
| ------------------- | --------------------------------- |
| 💰 Transaction Amount | $44.5M total |
| 📈 Interest Earned | ~$7.8M |
| 🧾 Annual Fees | ~$2.95M |
| 💳 Payment Methods | Swipe, Chip, Online |
| 🃏 Card Categories | Blue, Silver, Gold, Platinum |

### 2. 👥 Customer Dataset
Includes customer demographic details:

| Feature | Details |
| ---------------- | --------------------------------- |
| 🎂 Age Group | Young, Middle-aged, Senior |
| 💼 Job Type | Business, Executive, etc. |
| 🎓 Education Level | Graduate, Post-Graduate, etc. |
| 💰 Income Category | Low, Medium, High |

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 💰 Financial Performance
- 🥇 **Top Card Category:** Blue Card contributes **$46M (~80% of revenue)**
- 💳 **Most Used Payment Method:** Swipe (**$35M**)
- 📱 **Lowest Usage:** Online (**$3M**)
- 🧾 **Top Spending Category:** Bills (**$14M**)

### 👥 Customer Insights
- 🎓 **Top Education Segment:** Graduates (**$22M revenue**)
- 💼 **Top Professions:** Business owners & executives (**$17M**)
- 🎂 **Most Active Age Group:** 40–50 years
- 💎 **High Revenue Contributors:** High-income customers

### 🌍 Regional Performance
- Top-performing regions:
  - 🏆 Texas (TX)
  - 🥈 New York (NY)
  - 🥉 California (CA)

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### 🎯 1. Targeted Marketing
- Focus campaigns on:
  - 🎓 Graduates
  - 💼 Business professionals
  - 💰 High-income customers
  - 📍 Regions: TX, NY, CA

### 📱 2. Promote Digital Transactions
- Online transactions are significantly low (**$3M vs $35M Swipe**)
- Introduce to boost digital usage:
  - 💵 Cashback offers
  - 👛 Digital wallet rewards
  - 🛒 E-commerce partnerships

### 🏆 3. Card Tier Strategy
- Strengthen **Blue Card** benefits (current revenue leader)
- Create upgrade pathways:
  - Blue → Silver → Gold → Platinum
  - Offer premium incentives for high spenders
